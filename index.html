Study Project AMT-OS

From clicking a button to playing it yourself

We're excited to introduce our pioneering web application, born from the enthusiasm and dedication of the “Automatic Music Transcription” study project that started in the summer semester of 2024 at the University of Osnabrück. Whether for digital music production, providing sheet music for orchestras, or for personal hobbies, the goal of our project is to enable an effortless way to record or upload audio files, which are then transformed into MIDI and MusicXML (MXL) formats using advanced machine learning techniques. As we continue to enhance this project as a Study Initiative , we invite you to join us on this creative journey. Your feedback, participation, and support are invaluable as we refine our tools and explore the future of music together.
